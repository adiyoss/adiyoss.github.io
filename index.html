Yossi Adi

yossiadidrum at gmail dot com

My name is Yossi Adi, I'm a machine learning researcher and professional drummer. Currently, I'm pursuing my Ph.D in computer science at Bar-Ilan University under the supervision of Dr. Joseph Keshet.

My research interests are focused on developing and analyzing machine learning and deep learning algorithms for speech and language applications. I completed my M.Sc. (2015) in Computer Science at Bar-Ilan University. My complete CV can be found here.

As a musician, I'm the drummer and one of the band members of Lucille Crew.
