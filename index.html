My name in Yossi Adi, I'm a machine learning researcher and professional drummer. Currently, I'm pursuing my Ph.D in computer science at Bar-Ilan University under the supervision of Dr. Joseph Keshet. My research interests are focused on developing and analyzing machine learning and deep learning algorithms for speech and language.
As a musician I'm the drummer and one of the band members of Lucille Crew.
